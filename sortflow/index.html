Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

King Puzzle Games ("we", "our", or "us") built SortFlow - Ball Sort Puzzle Game ("the App") as a free game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Account Information (Optional): If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email, and profile picture to create your game profile. Sign-in is completely optional.

Game Data: When signed in, we store your game progress, scores, achievements, and preferences on our servers to provide cloud save and leaderboard functionality.

Without Sign-in: If you play without signing in, all data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To display leaderboard rankings
  • To provide daily challenges and rewards
  • To process in-app purchases
  • To send optional game notifications (with your permission)

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game progress (scores, levels completed, achievements)
  • Game statistics (total games played, moves, best scores)
  • User preferences (sound settings, theme preferences, language)

If you uninstall the App, locally stored data is automatically deleted.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, cloud database for leaderboards and cloud saves
  • Google Play Services — Google Sign-In and in-app purchases

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is suitable for all ages, and sign-in is optional. No advertising is shown in the App.

Your Rights

  • Play the game without signing in (no data collection)
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Opt out of notifications through your device settings

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
